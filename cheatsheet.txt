Draw elements
-------------
Point: SDL_RenderDrawPoint(renderer, x, y);
Line: SDL_RenderDrawLine(renderer, x1, y1, x2, y2);


Render
------
Clear: SDL_RenderClear(renderer);
Color: SDL_SetRenderDrawColor(renderer, r, g, b, alpha);
Render: SDL_RenderPresent(renderer);

Shapes
------
Point: SDL_Point(x, y);
